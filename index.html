about us

Kcycle was Established in 1994 by Sam Marco, who today is still managing the company. At Kcycle we understand that everyone is different and all driving lessons are tailored to suit your individual needs. With Kcycle you can be confident that you will learn to drive in a safe, friendly and comfortable environment. Kcycle Motoring School is also a Member of the M.S.A (MOTORING SCHOOLS ASSOCIATION).

Choosing the right Motoring School and Instructors is the most important thing to consider before booking your first driving lesson. The right Motoring School will ensure that you will be provided with:


- Professional Qualified Tutors
- Dual Controlled Vehicles
- Individual tuition
